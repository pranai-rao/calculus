Area Formulas

Area of a triangle: \(A=\frac{1}{2}bh\) or \(A=\frac{1}{2}ab\sin(c)\)

Quadrilaterals

Area of a square: \(A=b^2\)

Area of a rectangle: \(A=bh\)

Area of a trapezoid: \(A=\frac{1}{2}h(b_1+b_2)\)

Circles

Area of a circle: \(A=\pi r^2\)

Area of a sector: \(A=\frac{\theta}{2}r^2\)

Volume Formulas

Volume of a cyllinder: \(\pi r^2h\)

Volume of a rectangular prism: \(whl\)

Volume of a cube: \(b^3\)

Volume of a sphere: \(\frac{4}{3}\pi r^3\)

Volume of a cone: \(\frac{1}{3}\pi r^2h\)

Integrals

Indefinite integral: \(\int f(x)dx=F(x)+C\)

Constant Multiple Rule: \(\int kf(x)dx=k\int f(x)dx=kF(x)+C\)

Sum and Difference Rule: \(\int[f(x)\pm g(x)]dx=\int f(x)dx\pm \int g(x)dx\)

Area under the Curve

$$A=\lim_{n\to\infty}\sum_{k=1}^nf(x_k^*)\Delta x=\int_a^bf(x)dx$$

Differentiation

Sum and Difference Rule

\(\frac{d}{dx}[f(x)\pm g(x)]=f'(x)\pm g'(x)\)

Product Rule

\(\frac{d}{dx}f(x)g(x)=f'(x)g(x)+f(x)g'(x)\)

Quotient Rule

\(\frac{d}{dx}\frac{f(x)}{g(x)}=\frac{f'(x)g(x)-f(x)g'(x)}{(g(x))^2}\)

Power Rule

\(\frac{d}{dx}x^n=nx^{n-1}\)

Exponent Rule

\(\frac{d}{dx}b^x=b^x\cdot\ln(b)\)

Logarithmic Rule

\(\frac{d}{dx}\log_b(x)=\frac{1}{x\ln(b)}\)

Chain Rule

\(\frac{d}{dx}f(g(x))=f'(g(x))\cdot g'(x)\)
